What is Probinsya-Know?

Probinsya-Know is an informative travel website that lets people explore and know more about the provinces in the Philippines. While most people know about the places like Vigan, Boracay, Davao, and Manila, this website showcases the hidden wonders of the Philippine archipelago such as the mountains of Kalinga, the island of Marinduque and many more. This website provides images and information about the beautiful landmarks and rich culture of each province. This website also shows the history, and fun facts about the province. While you are at home, let us explore and discover the Philippines! (Pictures on the left: Mt. Pulag, Quezon, Negros Occidental, Siquijor, Dinagat Islands, and Batanes)



The Philippines

The Philippines is an archipelagic country found in Southeast-Asia. It is home to over 100 million people found on its more than 7000 unique islands that is divided into three groups: Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. The Philippines is a megadiverse country and has hundreds of natural wonders such as mountains, beaches, rivers, and lakes. (Map from itsmorefuninthephilippines.co.uk)
